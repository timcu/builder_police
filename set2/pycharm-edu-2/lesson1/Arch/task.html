Arch - hollow

That wall doesn't look like an arch!

Task

Remove the centre part of the wall so it becomes an arch. Remember for the range_y_int and range_z_int you can use a list of numbers or a formula.

range_y_int = [10, 11, 12, 13, 14]

or

range_y_int = range(floor_y + 1, floor_y + arch_height - 1)

Lesson

To remove blocks we replace them with "air".

In your program, the external dimensions are for the glass blocks on the outside of the arch range_y_ext, range_z_ext. The internal dimensions are for the air blocks on the inside of the arch range_y_int, range_z_int.
